Welcome to Vivante

Vivante at Mahim is a great place to live, offering a perfect blend of urban convenience, luxury and tranquillity. It boasts of excellent connectivity to South Mumbai, Western Express Highway, BKC and the western suburbs.

There is an abundance of reputed schools, hospitals, temples, cinemas, malls, and vibrant markets. With adequate open space, a safe environment, and a thriving community, it provides a comfortable lifestyle for modern families and professionals alike.
